WHY SDGs STUDIO?

Nepal Government has prepared the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) Roadmap 2016-2030 and developed the 15th Development Plan inline with SDGs. Nepal government has developed the strategies for localizing SDGs in local government. But, there is still need to disseminate the right information at local level to ensure the development inline with SDGs therefore SDGs Studio is disseminating the right information on SDGs from Federal to local level as well as contribute to promote the SDGs in Nepal. SDGs Studio is doing partnership with government, private and non-governmental organization to promote the SDGs because SDGs Studio covers the mass population through Television, YouTube and other forms of media.
