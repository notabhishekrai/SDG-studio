About SDGs STUDIO

Introduction

Nepal Government has prepared the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) status and roadmap 2016-2030 and developed the 15th Development Plan inline with SDGs. Nepal government has developed the strategies for localizing SDGs in local level. Though the Nepal government has the plan to localize the SDGs inlining with planning process but still there is gap of proper knowledge and right information on SDGs and its localization. So, to fulfill that gap SDGs Studio is registered in 2017 AD with registration no. 173830/074/075 in the Ministry of Industry, Government of Nepal, pursuant to sub-section (1) of section 5 of the Companies Act, 2006. It is the first ever organization established with the motive of localizing global agendas through the use of different forms of Media, Networking, Lobby Advocacy, Capacity Building, Research and Publications with partnering and Promoting PPP (Public Private Partnership). SDGs Studio also has its own studio room for producing the TV programs with having latest technologically sound equipment along with capacitated and trained human resources.

Mission

SDG Studio brings forth global and local agendas on girls' empowerment, gender equality and social justice, Civic Education, Human Rights, through visualization and documentation with their real-life stories, experiences. Besides this, SDG Studio is developing behavior change communications materials related to empowerment as well as localization SDGs. Currently, SDG Studio is producing and developing the following programs in coordination with government agencies, development partners and corporate sectors.

  • We work for the localization of SDGs goals.
  • We promote GESI.
  • We promote youth engagement.
  • We promote Youth Entrepreneurship .
