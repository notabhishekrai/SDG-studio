Pradeep Lamichhane

Expertise on Child Rights & GESI

On the surface, Mr. Pradeep Kumar Lamichhane Chairman of SDG Studio is a Social Activist with over 10 plus years of experience in social field in Nepal. He was born in remote village of Syangja District and grew up in Palpa District of Nepal. At the age of 13 he establishes one child club named Srijansil Child Club in his initiation and he serve there as a founder president for 3 year which shows his interest in social work from the childhood. We know Mr. Lamichhane as trainer (life skills, master of ceremony and child rights), Facilitator, researcher, content writer, director, program developer and manager as well. He has strong background in working with children, adolescents, youths, women, Dalit, marginalize and backward communities in Nepal. He is simple positive person with learning attitude and pleasant personality. He believes in team work and love to work in network with new peoples. Mr. Lamichhane is currently involved in Hatemalo Sanchar a child rights organization as a program Coordinator, Consortium of organizations working for child participation (Consortium-Nepal) as a Secretary, Nepal Dalit Human Rights Organization (DHR-Nepal) as a Secretary. In past he has worked as Volunteer Teacher in Rasuwa District 2009, which is one of the remote Himalayan regions of Nepal.
